Our Story

Built on family.
Driven by purpose.

MASS Collective grew from a single family restaurant into a hospitality group guided by the same principles that started it all — genuine care for people, deep respect for our Pakistani heritage, and a commitment to the communities around us.

For over two decades, we've been serving the Greater Seattle area, growing from Naan N Curry into a collective of brands that each carry our values forward.
